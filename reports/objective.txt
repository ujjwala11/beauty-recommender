Project Objective
Business Goal

Develop an AI Beauty Intelligence Platform that provides:

Personalized beauty product recommendations
Explainable recommendations
Personalized AM/PM skincare routines
Seasonal trend analytics
Product similarity search
Ingredient intelligence
ML Objectives

We are solving multiple ML problems.

Problem	Type	Evaluation
Predict product rating	Regression	RMSE, MAE, R²
Predict recommendation (Yes/No)	Classification	ROC-AUC, F1, Precision, Recall
Review sentiment	NLP Classification	F1, Accuracy
Recommend products	Recommendation	Precision@K, Recall@K, NDCG
Product similarity	Information Retrieval	Precision@K

Notice how each problem has its own appropriate evaluation metrics.
